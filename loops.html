Loops

Programs often need to repeat some action. A portion of a program that repeats a statement or group of statemetns is called a loop. The statement or group of statements in a loop is called the body of the loop Java has three> standard looping constructs: while do-while, and for.
The key to a loop is the conditional test. In Java, a conditional test is an expression that results in a boolean value - in other words, something that is either true or false.
